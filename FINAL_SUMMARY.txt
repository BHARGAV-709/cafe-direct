💻 HOW TO USE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Start the development server:
  $ cd /vercel/share/v0-project
  $ pnpm dev

Open in browser:
  http://localhost:3000

Test the features:
  1. Browse menu items (with food pictures!)
  2. Click Add to Cart - see "Added!" animation
  3. Click category filters - watch smooth transitions
  4. Click heart icon - toggle favorite state
  5. Go to cart - see quantity controls
  6. Click + to increase quantity - smooth animation
  7. Click - to decrease quantity - smooth animation
  8. Click X to remove - item deleted instantly
  9. Enter name & phone
  10. Click "Order via WhatsApp" - pre-filled message opens
